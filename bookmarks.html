Octopuses Have Three Hearts

Octopuses are fascinating creatures – one of the coolest facts is that they have three hearts! Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.

Bananas Are Berries

It might sound surprising, but bananas are actually classified as berries in the botanical world. They develop from a flower with a single ovary and have a soft, fleshy fruit.
